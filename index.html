Indextoz Corporation™ (I²c™) is a digital-first corporation specializing in the creation of interactive HTML content and games. The company was founded on August 1st, 2024, by Andrew Dexinoz from Vietnam and Winston Dexinoz from Russia. Prior to April 1st, 2025, it operated under the name Indextoz Interactive Corporation™.

History
Indextoz Corporation was founded with the goal of creating immersive, interactive experiences within the digital realm. Established by Andrew and Winston Dexinoz, the corporation’s focus has always been on digital engagement, providing online experiences that blend entertainment and interaction.

Initially operating under the name Indextoz Interactive Corporation™, the company underwent a rebrand to Indextoz Corporation™ on April 1st, 2025, to align with its evolving digital presence. The rebranding marked a new chapter as the corporation continued to expand its digital services and offerings.

Focus and Operations
Indextoz Corporation operates entirely in the digital space, offering a range of interactive HTML-based experiences, including games and other forms of web-based engagement. The company does not engage in the development of physical technology but instead focuses on enhancing user interaction through innovative, online platforms and digital products.

Indextoz’s primary mission is to create dynamic and entertaining web experiences that engage users in new and creative ways, leveraging the power of the internet and HTML-based content. The company has quickly established itself as a digital entity, focusing on virtual environments and online interactivity.

Founders
  • Andrew Dexinoz – A digital entrepreneur from Vietnam, Andrew has a background in web development and online media. He plays a key role in shaping the company's digital-first philosophy.
  • Winston Dexinoz – A developer from Russia, Winston is focused on the programming and design aspects of the corporation's interactive products, particularly in gaming and web content.
Corporate Evolution
Since its founding, Indextoz Corporation has maintained a completely digital presence. The corporation's approach revolves around creating web-based interactive experiences that engage and entertain. By operating solely in the digital domain, Indextoz has avoided the complexities of physical tech development, choosing instead to focus on creating content that exists entirely online.
